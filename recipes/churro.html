Churro

Home

Description

Churros are a delicious, fried pastry dusted with sugar and

cinammon. They can be served with melted chocolate to be a perfect sweet treat!

Ingredients

(For 12 churros)

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1/8 tsp. salt
  • 2 tbsp. butter
  • 1 cup (140g) flour sifted
  • 1 large egg beaten
  • 2 1/2 cups frying oil
  • Sugar for dusting
  • 1 tsp. ground cinammon for dusting

Steps

  1. Make Dough. Heat the water, vanilla extract, salt, and butter in saucepan over medium-high heat.
  2. Once the mix is at a rolling boil, stir in flour all at once.
  3. Mix dough vigorously using a wooden spoon very quickly.
  4. Take the pan off heat and let cool for about a minute. Add the egg, and mix until the egg is completely incorporated into the dough. The dough should be smooth and soft, and should seperate from the bottom of the pot.
  5. Prep Pastry Bag. Place the dough in the pastry bag fitted with a star-shaped tip. There should be no air bubbles in the dough.
  6. Pipe out 6-inch strips on a tray covered with parchement paper. Also, prepare a plate or tray with paper towels to place the churro on after. Do not touch the churros or oil after they are bubbling in the oil.
  7. Pipe and Cook. Fry the churros for about 2 minutes in 320F oil or until golden brown. Turn and repeat for even color.
  8. Once done, remove from heat and place on prepared plate to drain for a few seconds, and then immediately roll in sugar and cinammon mix.
  9. Serve with melted chocolate.
  10. Enjoy!
